## FCC Proposes End to Anonymous 'Burner' Phones

The FCC is moving to eliminate anonymous mobile phone plans by requiring government IDs for all users. While framed as a fight against scammers, the move is condemned by privacy advocates as a 'dystopian' step toward state-sponsored surveillance that endangers domestic abuse survivors and journalists.
