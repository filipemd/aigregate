## Polymarket's Fake Win Campaign

Polymarket paid social media creators to film fake bets on cloned websites to lure US users into its platform, despite being largely barred in the US. An investigation found over 1,100 videos showing $1.9 million in simulated wins that never occurred on the actual exchange.

- [https://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/2026/06/polymarkets-viral-videos-showed-people-winning-big-but-the-bets-were-fake/](https://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/2026/06/polymarkets-viral-videos-showed-people-winning-big-but-the-bets-were-fake/)

## The AI Affordability Crisis

AI platforms are transitioning from heavily subsidized subscription models to expensive token-based billing as losses mount. With OpenAI reporting a $38.5 billion net loss in 2025, the industry faces a 'sucker farming' cycle where massive debt may force the rapid displacement of millions of human jobs to achieve profitability.

- [https://blog.dshr.org/2026/06/ais-affordability-crisis.html](https://blog.dshr.org/2026/06/ais-affordability-crisis.html)

## OpenAI's Daybreak Cyber Defense

OpenAI launched Daybreak, a suite of tools including GPT-5.5-Cyber and Codex Security, designed to shift the cybersecurity bottleneck from finding vulnerabilities to patching them. The initiative includes 'Patch the Planet,' which uses AI to help open-source maintainers fix critical flaws in projects like Python and Go.

- [https://openai.com/index/daybreak-securing-the-world/](https://openai.com/index/daybreak-securing-the-world/)

## MSG's Activist Dossiers

## Claude Tag: AI as a Teammate

Anthropic introduced Claude Tag, allowing teams to @mention Claude in Slack to delegate complex tasks. Unlike standard chatbots, Claude Tag is 'multiplayer,' remembering channel context over time and taking proactive initiative to update teams on unresolved tasks.

- [https://www.anthropic.com/news/introducing-claude-tag](https://www.anthropic.com/news/introducing-claude-tag)

## GLM-5.2: SOTA Open Model

Z.ai released GLM-5.2, a massive open-weight model that rivals the top proprietary LLMs in coding and reasoning. Through Unsloth's dynamic quantization, the model can now be run locally on high-end consumer hardware, such as a 256GB Mac.

- [https://unsloth.ai/docs/models/glm-5.2](https://unsloth.ai/docs/models/glm-5.2)

## Linux Secure Boot Crisis

A critical Microsoft key used to boot Linux distributions with Secure Boot enabled is set to expire in September. This may prevent new Linux installations from booting on millions of systems unless hardware vendors issue firmware updates or users manually disable Secure Boot.

- [https://lwn.net/Articles/1029767/](https://lwn.net/Articles/1029767/)
