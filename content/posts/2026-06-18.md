## Arch Linux AUR Malware Crisis

Arch Linux has blocked new AUR registrations after a massive malware injection affecting over 1,500 packages. The community is now debating a fundamental rebuild of the repository, including the implementation of user trust scores and vetted community repos to prevent future poisoning.

## Fedora's Packager Exodus

Fedora Project Leader Jef Spaleta reported an alarming decline in active packagers, dropping from over 420 in 2020 to roughly 325 in 2026. To combat this, the project is moving toward a GitHub-like 'software forge' (Fedora Forge) and exploring new donation frameworks to expand resources beyond Red Hat's budget.

## Fedora's Secret 'Hummingbird' Project

Fedora has launched 'Hummingbird,' a container-based rolling distribution, but the project has faced internal backlash because the initiative was approved by the council in secret to expedite its announcement at the Red Hat Summit, bypassing standard public ticket processes.
