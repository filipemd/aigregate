## Meta's AI Training Surveillance

Meta is reportedly installing surveillance software on employee computers to record keystrokes, mouse movements, and screenshots. The data is intended to train AI agents to understand how humans interact with computers to automate professional workflows.

## Anthropic's Mythos: AI-Driven Bug Hunting

## Apple Fixes iPhone Privacy Leak

Apple patched a vulnerability that allowed law enforcement to extract deleted or disappearing messages from apps like Signal. The bug occurred because the OS cached notification content for up to a month, bypassing the app's own deletion timers.

- [https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/22/apple-fixes-bug-that-cops-used-to-extract-deleted-chat-messages-from-iphones/](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/22/apple-fixes-bug-that-cops-used-to-extract-deleted-chat-messages-from-iphones/)

## Thiel's AI Tribunal for Journalists

Peter Thiel has funded Objection.ai, a startup that uses AI and former intelligence agents to challenge media reporting. The platform aims to provide a low-cost way to harass journalists by issuing AI-generated verdicts and 'trust scores' outside the traditional legal system.

- [https://www.codastory.com/polarization/can-we-trust-an-ai-jury-to-judge-journalism/](https://www.codastory.com/polarization/can-we-trust-an-ai-jury-to-judge-journalism/)

## CATL's Ultra-Fast EV Battery

CATL unveiled a new LFP battery capable of charging from 10% to 98% in under 7 minutes. The battery maintains high performance in extreme cold, charging to 98% in 9 minutes even at -22°F.

- [https://arstechnica.com/cars/2026/04/catls-new-lfp-battery-can-charge-from-10-to-98-in-less-than-7-minutes/](https://arstechnica.com/cars/2026/04/catls-new-lfp-battery-can-charge-from-10-to-98-in-less-than-7-minutes/)

## Claude Desktop 'Spyware' Allegations

Anthropic's Claude Desktop for macOS is accused of acting as 'spyware' by silently installing configuration files that grant the app access to various web browsers, including those not yet installed on the user's system, potentially violating EU privacy laws.

- [https://www.theregister.com/2026/04/20/anthropic_claude_desktop_spyware_allegation/](https://www.theregister.com/2026/04/20/anthropic_claude_desktop_spyware_allegation/)

## Google's 8th Gen TPU for AI Agents

Google launched its eighth-generation TPUs, specifically optimized for the 'agentic era.' The TPU 8i focuses on reducing latency for AI agents that reason in continuous loops, while the TPU 8t accelerates the development of massive frontier models.

- [https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/infrastructure-and-cloud/google-cloud/eighth-generation-tpu-agentic-era/](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/infrastructure-and-cloud/google-cloud/eighth-generation-tpu-agentic-era/)
