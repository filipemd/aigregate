## China Sets New Fiber-Optic Speed Record

Chinese researchers have achieved a massive leap in internet speed by transmitting 51.3Tb/s through a hollow-core fiber cable. Unlike traditional glass fiber, this technology guides light through air to reduce latency and boost capacity for next-gen backbone infrastructure.

## Microsoft Launches Its Own Cloud-Only Linux Distro
