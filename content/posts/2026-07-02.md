## PS5 Now Supports Linux

The ps5-linux project has successfully expanded its support to newer firmware versions and the PS5 Slim, effectively turning the console into a Linux-based gaming rig. The project now supports 4K60 video/audio output, M.2 SSD partitions, and full USB, Bluetooth, and Ethernet connectivity. However, support for firmware version 8.00 and above is currently not supported.
