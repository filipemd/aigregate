## Global RAM Shortage May Last Until 2030

Consumer electronics prices for phones, laptops, and VR headsets are rising as memory makers are expected to meet only 60% of demand by 2027. The shortage is driven by the prioritization of AI-specific HBM over standard RAM, with some industry leaders warning the crisis could persist until 2030.
