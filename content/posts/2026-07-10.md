## The Bot-Infested Internet

A website owner reports trapping 50 million bots using 'digital tar pits'—traps that feed AI scrapers nonsensical data to poison training sets. The author estimates that bot traffic may actually exceed 70% of all internet activity, far higher than official estimates, suggesting the 'Dead Internet Theory' is becoming a reality.
