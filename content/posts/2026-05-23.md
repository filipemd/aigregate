## AI Memory Crunch Kills Cheap Smartphones

The era of ultra-cheap smartphones is ending as AI demand reallocates global memory production. Because HBM (High-Bandwidth Memory) required for AI chips consumes three times the wafer capacity of standard mobile memory, manufacturers are prioritizing high-margin AI hardware, pricing the world's poorest populations out of basic computing access.
